Meet Melissa...

Known for her authenticity, warmth and engaging personality, Melissa has the ability to help individuals gain deeper self-awareness, more meaningful connections, and show up in a loving manner towards themselves and others. Her rich personal journey has included highs and lows of life, and she believes this adds to her ability to understand and help others.

Take a few moments to reflect on what brought you to this website.

“Are you open and ready to discover yourself with new awareness?

I would be honoured to be a part of your journey of self-exploration, integration, and healing. Together we will take the steps necessary towards attaining your life goals and becoming your most whole, authentic self.”

Melissa
