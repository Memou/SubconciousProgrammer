INDIVIDUAL SESSIONS

Subconcious Programming is a dynamic approach to working with subconcious and our deeper aspects, combining a blend of energy healing,shadow work and body awareness. This combination helps create a healthy balance between mind, body, and spirit.

I recognize that each individual is uniquely beautiful and has the birthright to feel safe, seen, and fully heard. My compassion, understanding, and curiosity can help create a safe space for you to explore who you are, bring awareness to existing unhealthy patterns, and create a positive transformation that is in alignment with your core values.
My sessions are informal, experiential, holistic, and use evidence-based interventions. We will work collaboratively to design a treatment plan that is customized to your personal needs and works to ensure that we are moving forward in a way that activates growth. You will also be equipped with daily skills and tools that will help empower you to show up more authentically.
